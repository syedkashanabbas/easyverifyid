Companies House has mandated that obtaining a Personal Verification Code through an identity verification process will become a legal requirement for all company directors and persons with significant control (PSCs). This applies to both UK residents and non-UK residents alike, ensuring proper identification for all individuals holding key positions in UK companies.

As a registered Authorised Corporate Service Provider (ACSP) with Companies House, EasyVerifyID is officially authorised to facilitate the acquisition of Companies House Personal Codes by conducting thorough identity verification checks on company directors and persons of significant control, regardless of whether they are existing clients. These new verification codes will be essential for directors and PSCs to update their information or submit documentation in the future. These codes are distinct from the existing Companies House web filing codes currently in use. View the complete list of required identification documents for verification.

Failure to complete identity verification with Companies House and obtain your personal code may result in serious consequences:

  • Inability to submit mandatory filings such as confirmation statements
  • Risk of company dissolution or strike-off
  • Potential legal prosecution
  • Possible financial penalties
  • Potential disqualification from acting as a director in the UK
